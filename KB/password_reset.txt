Title: Resetting Your Company Password

Employees are required to change their passwords every 90 days to maintain security. To reset your company password:

1. Go to the password management portal.
2. Click on “Forgot Password” if you cannot recall your current password.
3. Enter your username and registered email address.
4. Follow the email instructions to create a new password. Ensure it meets the company’s complexity requirements: at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
5. Log in with your new password and update it in all necessary applications.

If you experience difficulties during the reset process, contact the IT helpdesk for assistance. Do not share your password with anyone.
