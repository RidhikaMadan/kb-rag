Title: Submitting a Travel Request

Employees needing to travel for business must follow the company travel approval process:

1. Log into the operations portal and select “Travel Requests.”
2. Enter travel dates, purpose, destination, and estimated expenses.
3. Attach supporting documents, such as meeting invitations or event agendas.
4. Submit the request for managerial and finance approval.
5. Once approved, the travel coordinator will assist with bookings and reimbursement instructions.

All travel must comply with company travel policies. Requests submitted less than one week before departure may not be approved.
