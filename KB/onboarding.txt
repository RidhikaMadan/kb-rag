Title: New Employee Onboarding Guide

Our onboarding process is designed to help new employees integrate smoothly into the company. The onboarding steps include:

1. Complete all required HR forms, including tax documents, benefits enrollment, and emergency contacts.
2. Attend the company orientation session, where you will be introduced to company policies, mission, and values.
3. Set up your company accounts (email, internal portal, software tools) with guidance from the IT department.
4. Review your department-specific training materials and initial project assignments.
5. Meet with your manager for a one-on-one session to clarify role expectations and goals for the first 90 days.

Throughout the onboarding process, you will have access to a mentor or buddy who can answer questions and provide guidance. The HR team is available to address any concerns during the onboarding period.
