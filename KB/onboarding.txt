COMPREHENSIVE NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE

Welcome to TechCorp! This comprehensive onboarding guide will help you navigate your first weeks and months with our organization. Our onboarding process is designed to ensure you have all the tools, knowledge, and connections needed to succeed.

PRE-ARRIVAL CHECKLIST (Complete Before Day 1)
- Review and complete all HR forms sent via email (tax documents, direct deposit, emergency contacts)
- Download and install required software from the IT portal (VPN client, communication tools, development environments)
- Review the employee handbook and code of conduct
- Set up your company email account and calendar access
- Complete background check and drug screening if required for your role

FIRST DAY ACTIVITIES
Your first day begins at 9:00 AM in the main conference room for company orientation. You will:
1. Meet with HR for final paperwork and badge creation
2. Attend company-wide orientation covering:
   - Company history, mission, and values
   - Organizational structure and key leadership
   - Benefits overview and enrollment process
   - IT security policies and acceptable use guidelines
   - Workplace safety protocols
3. Receive your equipment package (laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse, headset)
4. Meet your assigned onboarding buddy who will guide you through your first month
5. Have lunch with your team and manager

WEEK 1 OBJECTIVES
- Complete all required training modules in the learning management system (LMS)
- Set up your development environment and access necessary repositories
- Review your role description and initial project assignments
- Schedule one-on-one meetings with key team members
- Familiarize yourself with company communication tools (Slack, Microsoft Teams, Jira)
- Complete security awareness training and data protection certification

FIRST 30 DAYS MILESTONES
By the end of your first month, you should:
- Have completed all mandatory training courses
- Understand your team's workflow and contribution expectations
- Have access to all necessary systems and tools
- Completed benefits enrollment (deadline: 30 days from start date)
- Met with your manager for your first performance check-in
- Contributed to at least one team project or task

ONBOARDING SUPPORT RESOURCES
- HR Department: hr@techcorp.com, ext. 1000
- IT Helpdesk: helpdesk@techcorp.com, ext. 2000
- Your Onboarding Buddy: Assigned on day one
- Manager: Schedule weekly check-ins for first 90 days
- Employee Resource Groups: Join relevant ERGs for networking and support

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Q: When do I get my first paycheck?
A: Payroll runs bi-weekly. Your first check will arrive on the second payday after your start date.

Q: Can I work remotely?
A: We offer hybrid work arrangements. Discuss your schedule preferences with your manager during week one.

Q: What if I have questions after hours?
A: Your onboarding buddy and manager are available during business hours. For urgent IT issues, contact the 24/7 helpdesk.

Q: When can I start contributing to projects?
A: Most new employees begin contributing to small tasks within their first week, with full project involvement by week 3-4.

Remember: There's no such thing as a silly question during onboarding. We're here to help you succeed!
