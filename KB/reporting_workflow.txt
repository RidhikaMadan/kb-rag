Title: Internal Reporting Workflow

To ensure smooth operations, employees must follow the internal reporting workflow for project updates and status reports:

1. Log into the project management system.
2. Update your tasks and milestones daily, marking progress accurately.
3. Submit weekly status reports every Friday, summarizing completed tasks, ongoing work, and any blockers.
4. Send reports to your manager and relevant stakeholders.
5. Attend weekly team meetings to discuss reports, provide clarifications, and plan for the upcoming week.

Following this workflow ensures transparency, accountability, and efficient communication across teams. Any deviations or delays should be communicated proactively.
