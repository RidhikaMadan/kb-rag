Title: Requesting Vacation Leave

At our company, employees are encouraged to plan their vacation time in advance to ensure proper workflow coverage. To request vacation leave, follow these steps:

1. Review your available leave balance through the internal HR portal, Luminous. Ensure you have enough accrued days for your planned time off.
2. Discuss your intended leave dates with your immediate supervisor to confirm that your absence will not conflict with critical projects.
3. Submit a formal vacation request using the HR portal. Enter the start and end dates, reason for leave (optional), and any backup coverage arrangements.
4. Your supervisor will review the request and provide approval or request adjustments if necessary.
5. Once approved, HR will confirm the leave in your employee record.

Please note that requests submitted less than two weeks in advance may not be approved unless under exceptional circumstances. Additionally, employees should ensure that any ongoing tasks are delegated or postponed to avoid disruption.
