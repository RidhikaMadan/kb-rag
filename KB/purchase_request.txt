PURCHASE REQUEST AND PROCUREMENT PROCEDURES

OVERVIEW
All company purchases must follow the procurement process to ensure proper approval, budget compliance, and vendor management. This guide covers the complete process from request submission to order fulfillment.

PURCHASE REQUEST TYPES

Standard Purchase Requests:
- Office supplies and equipment
- Software licenses and subscriptions
- Professional services and consulting
- Marketing materials and promotional items
- Training and conference registrations

Capital Expenditures:
- Equipment over $5,000
- Furniture and fixtures
- Technology infrastructure
- Facility improvements

Emergency Purchases:
- Critical business operations
- Health and safety requirements
- Time-sensitive opportunities

SUBMITTING A PURCHASE REQUEST

Step 1: Pre-Request Preparation
Before submitting, gather:
- Item/service description and specifications
- Vendor information and contact details
- Quotes or pricing information (minimum 2-3 quotes for purchases over $1,000)
- Business justification
- Budget code or cost center
- Manager approval (verbal or email)

Step 2: Create Purchase Request
1. Log into Operations Portal at https://ops.techcorp.com
2. Navigate to "Procurement" > "Purchase Requests" > "New Request"
3. Complete all required fields:
   - Requester Information: Name, department, employee ID
   - Purchase Details:
     * Item/service description
     * Quantity
     * Unit price and total cost
     * Vendor name and contact information
     * Requested delivery date
   - Business Justification:
     * Why this purchase is needed
     * How it supports business objectives
     * Impact if not purchased
   - Budget Information:
     * Cost center or budget code
     * Project code (if applicable)
     * Funding source
   - Supporting Documents:
     * Vendor quotes (PDF format)
     * Product specifications
     * Manager approval email
     * Alternative vendor quotes (if required)

Step 3: Approval Workflow
Submit request and await approvals:
- Manager Approval: Required for all requests
- Finance Approval: Required for purchases over $500
- Department Head Approval: Required for purchases over $5,000
- Executive Approval: Required for purchases over $25,000

APPROVAL TIMELINES
- Standard requests (<$500): 2-3 business days
- Medium requests ($500-$5,000): 3-5 business days
- Large requests ($5,000-$25,000): 5-7 business days
- Capital expenditures (>$25,000): 7-10 business days
- Emergency requests: Expedited review, typically 24-48 hours

PURCHASE ORDER PROCESS

Once Approved:
1. You'll receive email confirmation with purchase order (PO) number
2. Procurement team contacts vendor with official PO
3. Vendor confirms order and provides delivery timeline
4. You receive updates on order status
5. Upon delivery, verify items match order
6. Submit receipt/invoice for payment processing

VENDOR REQUIREMENTS
- Preferred vendors: Use company-approved vendors when possible (see vendor list in portal)
- New vendors: Must complete vendor registration process
- Vendor evaluation: Based on price, quality, delivery, and service
- Payment terms: Net 30 standard, negotiate better terms when possible

BUDGET AND COST CENTERS
- All purchases must be charged to appropriate cost center
- Verify budget availability before submitting request
- Project-specific purchases require project code
- Capital expenditures require capital budget approval

PROHIBITED PURCHASES
The following cannot be purchased through company procurement:
- Personal items or services
- Gifts for personal use
- Alcohol (except approved business events)
- Items for resale
- Services from unregistered vendors
- Purchases exceeding budget without approval

EMERGENCY PURCHASES
For urgent business-critical purchases:
1. Contact Procurement Department immediately: procurement@techcorp.com, ext. 5000
2. Explain urgency and business impact
3. Get verbal approval from manager and finance
4. Submit formal request within 24 hours
5. Expedited processing available for true emergencies

RECEIVING AND VERIFICATION
When items are delivered:
1. Inspect packaging for damage
2. Verify items match purchase order
3. Check quantities and specifications
4. Test equipment if applicable
5. Report any discrepancies immediately
6. Sign delivery receipt
7. Update purchase request status in portal

RETURNS AND EXCHANGES
- Contact vendor directly for returns/exchanges
- Notify Procurement Department of returns
- Update purchase request with return information
- Process refunds through finance department

EXPENSE REPORTING FOR DIRECT PURCHASES
If you purchase directly (with prior approval):
1. Submit expense report with receipts
2. Include purchase order number if available
3. Attach itemized receipts
4. Categorize expense appropriately
5. Submit within 5 business days of purchase

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Can I purchase items directly and get reimbursed?
A: Only with prior approval and for small purchases (<$100). Submit request first, get approval, then purchase.

Q: How do I know if a vendor is approved?
A: Check the Preferred Vendors list in Operations Portal or contact Procurement.

Q: What if I need something urgently?
A: Contact Procurement for emergency purchase process. Standard process may be too slow.

Q: Can I change a purchase request after submission?
A: Yes, if not yet approved. Cancel and resubmit. If approved, contact Procurement for modifications.

Q: What if my request is denied?
A: Review denial reason. You can appeal with additional justification or resubmit with modifications.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Varies by vendor and item. Check with Procurement for estimated delivery times.

BEST PRACTICES
- Plan purchases in advance when possible
- Get multiple quotes for significant purchases
- Use preferred vendors for better pricing and service
- Provide clear business justification
- Keep all documentation organized
- Follow up on delivery status
- Report issues promptly
