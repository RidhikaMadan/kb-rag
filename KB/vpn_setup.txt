Title: VPN Setup Guide

To securely access company resources remotely, all employees must use the company VPN. Follow these steps to set up your VPN:

1. Download the official VPN client from the IT portal. Ensure you select the correct version for your operating system.
2. Install the client, following the on-screen instructions.
3. Open the VPN client and enter your company credentials.
4. Select the appropriate server location (usually your regional office) to optimize connection speed.
5. Test your connection by accessing internal resources such as the intranet or shared drives.

If you encounter any connection issues, contact the IT helpdesk with a description of the problem, your operating system, and screenshots if possible. Remember to disconnect from the VPN when not in use to maintain network security.
