Title: Submitting a Purchase Request

All purchase requests must be submitted through the company’s operations portal to ensure proper approval and tracking. Follow these steps:

1. Log into the operations portal with your employee credentials.
2. Navigate to “Purchase Requests” and click “Create New Request.”
3. Enter details of the items or services needed, including vendor, quantity, and estimated cost.
4. Attach any necessary supporting documents such as quotes or approvals from your manager.
5. Submit the request for approval. Your manager and the finance team will review and either approve, reject, or request modifications.

Once approved, the purchasing department will process the order and notify you when the items are ready or delivered. Keep records of all purchase requests for auditing purposes.
