COMPREHENSIVE BENEFITS ENROLLMENT GUIDE

OVERVIEW
TechCorp offers a comprehensive benefits package designed to support your health, financial security, and work-life balance. All eligible employees can enroll in benefits starting on their first day of employment. This guide will help you understand your options and complete enrollment successfully.

ELIGIBILITY
- Full-time employees: Eligible immediately upon hire
- Part-time employees (20+ hours/week): Eligible after 90 days
- Temporary employees: Not eligible for benefits
- Contractors: Not eligible for company benefits

ENROLLMENT PERIODS

New Hire Enrollment:
- Enrollment window: First 30 days of employment
- Coverage begins: First day of employment (if enrolled within 30 days)
- Late enrollment: Requires special approval and may have waiting periods

Annual Open Enrollment:
- Dates: November 1-15 each year
- Changes take effect: January 1 of following year
- All employees can make changes during this period

Qualifying Life Events (Special Enrollment):
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in spouse's employment
- Must enroll within 30 days of qualifying event

HEALTH INSURANCE OPTIONS

Medical Plans Available:
1. Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) Plan
   - Lower premiums, network restrictions
   - Primary care physician required
   - Referrals needed for specialists
   - Best for: Employees who prefer lower costs and don't mind network restrictions

2. Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) Plan
   - Higher premiums, more flexibility
   - No referrals required
   - Out-of-network coverage available (higher costs)
   - Best for: Employees who want flexibility and choice

3. High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with HSA
   - Lower premiums, higher deductibles
   - Health Savings Account (HSA) with company contribution
   - Tax advantages
   - Best for: Healthy employees who want to save for future medical expenses

Plan Comparison:
- Review plan summaries in HR Portal
- Compare premiums, deductibles, copays, and out-of-pocket maximums
- Consider your health needs and family situation
- Use the plan comparison tool in the enrollment system

DENTAL INSURANCE
- Preventive care: 100% covered (cleanings, exams, X-rays)
- Basic procedures: 80% covered (fillings, extractions)
- Major procedures: 50% covered (crowns, bridges, root canals)
- Annual maximum: $1,500 per year
- Orthodontia: Available for dependents under 19

VISION INSURANCE
- Annual eye exam: Covered 100%
- Frames: Allowance up to $150 every 24 months
- Lenses: Covered with copay
- Contact lenses: Allowance up to $150 every 24 months
- LASIK discount: 20% off through network providers

RETIREMENT SAVINGS - 401(K) PLAN
- Company match: 100% of first 3% of salary, 50% of next 2% (total 4% match on 5% contribution)
- Vesting: Immediate for employee contributions, 3-year graded vesting for employer match
- Investment options: 20+ mutual funds, target-date funds, index funds
- Contribution limits: Up to IRS maximum ($22,500 in 2023, plus catch-up for 50+)
- Enrollment: Can enroll anytime, changes take effect next pay period

LIFE INSURANCE
- Basic Life Insurance: 1x annual salary (company-paid)
- Supplemental Life Insurance: Up to 5x annual salary (employee-paid)
- Spouse/Dependent Coverage: Available at group rates
- Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D): Included with life insurance

DISABILITY INSURANCE
- Short-Term Disability: 60% of salary, up to 26 weeks (company-paid)
- Long-Term Disability: 60% of salary, up to age 65 (employee-paid, optional)
- Waiting period: 90 days for long-term disability

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS
- Flexible Spending Account (FSA): Pre-tax dollars for medical and dependent care expenses
- Health Savings Account (HSA): For HDHP participants, company contributes $500 annually
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Free counseling and support services
- Wellness Program: Gym discounts, health screenings, wellness challenges
- Tuition Reimbursement: Up to $5,250 per year for job-related education
- Professional Development: Conference attendance, certification programs
- Paid Time Off: Vacation, sick leave, personal days (varies by tenure)
- Parental Leave: 12 weeks paid leave for new parents
- Commuter Benefits: Pre-tax transit and parking expenses

ENROLLMENT PROCESS

Step 1: Access HR Portal
1. Navigate to https://hr.techcorp.com
2. Log in with your company credentials
3. Select "Benefits Enrollment" from the main menu

Step 2: Review Plan Options
1. Read plan summaries and comparison materials
2. Use the benefits calculator to estimate costs
3. Attend benefits information sessions (scheduled during enrollment periods)
4. Consult with HR benefits specialist if you have questions

Step 3: Enroll in Benefits
1. Select your medical plan (HMO, PPO, or HDHP)
2. Add dependents if applicable (spouse, children)
3. Choose dental and vision coverage
4. Enroll in 401(k) and set contribution percentage
5. Select optional benefits (supplemental life, long-term disability, etc.)
6. Set up FSA or HSA contributions if applicable

Step 4: Review and Submit
1. Review your selections summary
2. Verify dependent information is correct
3. Confirm beneficiary designations for life insurance and 401(k)
4. Submit enrollment
5. Print or save confirmation for your records

Step 5: Receive Confirmation
- You'll receive email confirmation within 24 hours
- Benefit cards and welcome packets mailed within 2 weeks
- Coverage effective dates confirmed in confirmation email

DEPENDENT ENROLLMENT
Eligible Dependents:
- Legal spouse
- Children under 26 (biological, adopted, stepchildren)
- Disabled dependents over 26 (with documentation)

Required Documentation:
- Marriage certificate (for spouse)
- Birth certificates (for children)
- Adoption papers (if applicable)
- Social Security numbers for all dependents

MAKING CHANGES AFTER ENROLLMENT
- During open enrollment: Make any changes
- Outside open enrollment: Only for qualifying life events
- 401(k) contributions: Can change anytime
- Beneficiary designations: Can update anytime

COST INFORMATION
- Medical premiums: Shared cost (company pays 80%, employee pays 20%)
- Dental/Vision: Employee-paid, group rates
- Life Insurance: Basic coverage company-paid, supplemental employee-paid
- 401(k): Employee contribution only (company match is free money!)

PREMIUM PAYMENT
- Premiums deducted from bi-weekly paycheck
- Pre-tax deductions reduce taxable income
- Review pay stub to verify deductions

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What if I miss the enrollment deadline?
A: Contact HR immediately. Late enrollment may be possible with justification, but coverage may be delayed.

Q: Can I opt out of medical coverage?
A: Yes, if you have other coverage. You must provide proof of alternative coverage.

Q: When does coverage begin?
A: First day of employment if enrolled within 30 days, or first of month following enrollment.

Q: Can I change my 401(k) contribution?
A: Yes, anytime through the HR Portal. Changes take effect next pay period.

Q: What happens to my benefits if I leave the company?
A: COBRA continuation coverage available for 18 months. 401(k) can be rolled over or left in plan.
