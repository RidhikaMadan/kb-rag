Title: Benefits Enrollment Guide

All employees are eligible for company benefits starting on their first day of employment. To enroll:

1. Access the HR portal and navigate to the “Benefits Enrollment” section.
2. Review available health, dental, and vision plans, along with retirement savings options.
3. Select the plans that suit your needs and fill in required personal information.
4. Submit your selections before the enrollment deadline, typically within the first 30 days of employment.
5. Confirm enrollment and download a copy of your benefits summary for future reference.

HR is available to answer questions regarding plan details, coverage, and eligibility. Late enrollment may require special approval.
