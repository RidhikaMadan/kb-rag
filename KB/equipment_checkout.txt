Title: Equipment Checkout Procedure

Employees may check out company equipment for temporary use. To do so:

1. Log into the operations portal and navigate to “Equipment Checkout.”
2. Select the item(s) required and specify the intended use and return date.
3. Submit the request for approval by your manager.
4. Once approved, pick up the equipment from the designated storage area and sign the checkout log.
5. Return the equipment by the specified date in good condition. Report any damage or malfunctions to the operations team.

Following this procedure ensures accountability and proper maintenance of company assets.
